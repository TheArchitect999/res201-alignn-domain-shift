# Operating Rules

- evidence first, prose second — no claim without a traceable result or citation
- one AI session per stage — do not mix stages (e.g., blueprinting + drafting) in a single session
- every major figure gets a figure memo before its section is drafted
- keep literature claims, implementation details, and findings in separate documents or sections — do not conflate them
- do not overwrite, regenerate, or clean up result artifacts from the repo
- do not invent numbers — all quantitative claims must come from logged experiment outputs
