# Final Paper Factory

This workspace is for turning completed repository evidence into paper-ready material without changing the underlying results. During the paper phase, result artifacts are read-only: do not edit, regenerate, overwrite, or "clean up" outputs that support the paper.

Source PDFs (ALIGNN paper, CGCNN paper, template, assignment briefs) live in `paper_sources/` at the repo root.

Work flows forward through the numbered stages: `00` → `07`. Do not draft before blueprinting, and do not move material backward.

## Folder Map

### `00_source_of_truth/`
Canonical paper-phase rules, locked evidence references, and other documents that define what the writing process must follow.

### `01_blueprints/`
High-level paper plans such as outline sketches, section architecture, argument flow, and target contribution framing.

### `02_figure_memos/`
Working notes for figures. Each major figure should have its own memo describing the claim, evidence source, construction logic, and caveats.

### `03_section_inputs/`
Structured inputs for writing sections, including evidence summaries, bullet-point claims, and scoped notes assembled before drafting.

### `04_drafts/`
Active draft material for sections or full-paper passes before review.

### `05_reviewed_drafts/`
Drafts that have already gone through a review pass and are being retained as checkpoints.

### `06_template_ready/`
Manuscript content prepared for transfer into a journal, conference, or thesis template with minimal restructuring.

### `07_final_qc/`
Final quality-control checklists, consistency reviews, submission checks, and last-pass validation notes.
